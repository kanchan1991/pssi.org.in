How do I become a member of the PSSI ?

Procedure

  • Create your account on pssi.org.in and fill your profile .
  • Once profile details are entered, click on 'Apply For Membership' button on your profile page.
  • One of the PSSI board members will validate your profile info and approve/disapprove it.
  • Once membership is approved, make the payment by following the steps outlined in the membership page
    • .
  • Once payment is received, you will be added to the pssi mailing list as a member.

  • You need to be an Indian citizen.

What are the benefits of being a PSSI member?

  1. Be able to attend the Annual General Meeting (AGM) every year and make your voice heard in making the society policies and in the conduct and organization of PyCon India conference.
  2. Be able to stand for elections to the PSSI board and influence changes from a position of power.
  3. Apart from these, PSSI provides a platform for exerting a very powerful influence on the use and adoption of Python programming language in academic & industrial areas. Being part of PSSI allows you to make your voice heard and be a prominent part of this influence.
