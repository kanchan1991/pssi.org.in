The PSSI has allocated a total budget of INR 1,00,000 per annum in order to support tutors conducting workshops across India in different colleges.

PSSI will cover transportation/Food costs of the Tutor conducting the workshop.

These guidelines will be effective in case college doesnâ€™t provide these facilities.

Transportation:
  1. Local
    1. For taxi/cab hired, the cost covered for one way is INR 600.
    2. For own vehicle, the cost covered is INR 500 as total fuel cost.
  2. Outstation
    1. Upto INR 3000 is covered for round trip in train - 3 Tier AC.
  3. Tutor must share receipt/bill with PSSI to reimburse the amount.
  4. In case of Multiple tutors in a college on the same day, all tutors need to plan for cab/taxi pooling and upto INR 1000 can be reimbursed one way.
  5. Support Volunteer
    1. All volunteers can plan for cab/taxi pooling and INR 1000 can be reimbursed one way.
Food:
  1. We will be reimbursing upto INR 200.
